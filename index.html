Hobbies and Skills

My hobbies are riding dirtbikes, racing BMX, cheer leading, and wake boarding. I enjoy doing all of these things because it gets my energy up and I am always excited to do them. When ever I go to the desert to ride my dirtbike, I always try to keep up with my brothers. My brothers are generally fast, but I am always right there with them. When I raced BMX I was in the top 3 for nationals in my age group. I think these are my favorite hobbies that me and my family do.
